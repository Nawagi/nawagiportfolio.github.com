Sweet Battle





About the game

Sweet Battle was developed for Windows and Android as the Digital Games course Final Project and is a mix of Angry Birds with Plants vs. Zombies. The player uses fruits with special powers to defeat the enemy boss before their base is destroyed or time runs out.

To get three stars, it's not enough to just beat the boss, though. You must also defeat its followers and destroy a good part of the enemy base. To help with this, more fruits are unlocked throughout the levels. Each one has a different ability and needs to be purchased within the level itself to be used.


My experience with the project

I would say it was my biggest challenge because I was still starting to learn C# and Unity when I had to program an entire game for the first time. And there was also the college deadline, which only increased my insecurity. But thankfully, it all worked out in the end.
