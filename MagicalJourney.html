Magical Journey

Available on Google Play



About the game

It's a side-scrolling game in which the character's goal is to cross the magical forest and its dangers to reach the Annual Azalea Festival.

In addition to the main mechanics that involve, among other things, jumping on vines and hitchhiking with other animals, the players can choose between different characters to accompany them on their journey. There are also several scenario customization options.

Developed for Android. After a few months of production, it was released in the first half of 2020.


Responsibilities
