Zaranah

Available on itch.io



Zaranah was selected for the CCXP Awards 2022 shortlist for Brazil's Best National Game category.



About the game

In this puzzle adventure game, the main character embarks on a journey to recover his lost memories.

The project was developed by two people in about a year and released in December 2021 for Windows.


Responsibilities

I programmed the entire game, including character movement, enemy logic, and a checkpoint system.
