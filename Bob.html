Bob, the Pumpkin

Available on itch.io



About the game, and my responsibilities

Developed in October 2021 during KickStart@Tapps, Tapps Games' program to develop new talent for the game industry.

We were asked to make two 3D mini-games, a karting and a spooky one, for a virtual pet game.

Apart from the see-through-the-wall shader, I did the programming part of spooky, a mini-game in which the player needs to prevent playful ghosts from scaring the character.


My experience with the program

The contact with other developers in a complete team helped me to better understand the role of professionals in each area of development.

It also helped me realize that the amount of tools I still have to learn is greater than I thought. And I had the opportunity to use two in this project: NavMesh and Shader Graph.

NavMesh was used to randomize the character's movement around the house, and with Shader Graph, I made the ghost transparency effect following one of Gabriel Aguiar Prod.'s tutorials.
